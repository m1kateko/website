Chasing Ten

An oath to relentlessly defy the perceived limits of my body, laying it all on the track as I chase 10 seconds.

Why Ten?

I’m running for my life. No really, I'm literally running for my life.

I’ve always known that I’m most optimal when I’m in my physical element but I feel most alive when I'm running.

I need not only be active but my body is begging for me to be explosive.

It comes from within.

Chasing Ten allows me to take my body to a place it's never been before.

Winning matters of course. But it won't just fall in my lap, I have to chase it.

I’m aiming to do the 100m in 10 seconds. Further to that, I’ll be gunning to do the 200m in 20 seconds.

However long it takes. This is my pledge.
