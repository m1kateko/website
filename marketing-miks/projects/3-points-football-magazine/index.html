3 Pts football magazine

Date: December 2017

Client: Myself

Brief: What does a final year student do after writing their final exams? Create a magazine obviosuly.

Staring down the barrel of unemployment, I needed to create a product that would show potential emoloyers the value I could add to their organisation.

Application: I created a mock magazine, mockazine if you will. Having had no experience, I took a crash course in Adobe InDesign and put 3 Pts together. I wrote every article and put together the design and layout of each page. To make it feel complete, I inserted ads and compiled a crossword puzzle. What a fun way to spend my summer.

Oh, and I got the job.
