The making of the Sundowns dynasty

Date: 2 January 2021

Client: The 18 Area

Brief: Mamelodi Sundowns have dominated the local football scene for a while, and that ascent was like nothing witnessed before. On the back of a three-peat, Abafana Bestyle were on the cusp of becoming the first club to win four league titles in succession.

The brief here was to create a visual content piece detailing how Sundowns got there and mapping out for how much longer the dynasty would continue.

Application: I wrote and produced a video which was published on YouTube. The writing of the script came first. This was followed by the design of the elements which took place within the Adobe Creative Cloud suite of apps.
