World Environment Day

Date: 5 June 2022

Client News24

Brief: As the world continues to weather climate challenges, the brief was to create a compelling communication focused on World Environment Day, highlighting the importance of environmental conservation and encouraging readers to take action towards sustainability.

Application: I created a comprehensive newsletter focused on World Environment Day. The newsletter aims to educate and inspire readers about environmental issues, encourage them to take action, and provide resources for further engagement. The content includes an op-ed, supporting articles, opinions and perspectives from various stakeholders, also not forgetting the graphic of the earth shedding a tear, designed by me.

Project: Happy World Environment Day?
